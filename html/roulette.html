roulette module

class roulette.Roulette(player: Player)

Bases: object

A class used to play the roulette.

Parameters:

  • player (Player) – The player who plays the game

  • bet_weights (tuple) – A tuple with weights of different bets (constant: (1, 1, 2, 8, 11, 17, 35) )

  • bet_names (tuple) – A tuple with names of different bets (constant: (“even/odd”, “eighteens”, “dozens”, “four numbers”, “three numbers”, “two numbers”, “one number”))

make_bet() → tuple

Makes a bet by the interaction with a user.

Returns:

A tuple of the type of the bet and a number of tokens to bet

Return type:

tuple

show_available_bets() → None

Shows available bets as a table.

start_roulette() → None

Operates the entire game by the if/elif/else conditions. Communicates with roulette’s player, spends his tokens, takes bet, draws a random number, shows it and adds tokens to the player’s account after the eventual win.
