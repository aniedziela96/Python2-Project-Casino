races module

class races.Races(player: Player)

Bases: object

A class used to represent races and operate whole races game.

Parameters:

  • names (tuple) – A tuple with names for runners (constant: (“Całeczka”, “Pochodniusia”, “Różniczka”, “Miareczka”, “Sumka”, “Dystrybuantka”, “Transformatka”, “Bazunia”, “Graniczka”, “Metryczka”, “Potęgusia”))

  • tracks (tuple) – A tuple with types of tracks (constant: (“flat”, “rising”, “sloping”))

  • converters (tuple) – A tuple with prize converters (constant: (4, 2, 1.5, 1.2, 1.1))

  • enter_price (int) – An enter price

  • gamblers (tuple) – A tuple with fake players (constant: (“Darek”, “Rysiek”, “Piotrek”, “Bodzio”))

  • player (Player) – The player who plays races
